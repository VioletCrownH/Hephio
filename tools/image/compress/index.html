Free Image Compression — Reduce File Size Without Losing Quality

Hephio's image compressor reduces the file size of your photos and graphics without sending them to a server. Everything happens locally in your browser, which means your files stay private and the tool works even without a fast internet connection.

Image compression is essential whenever you need to email photos, upload images to a website, or meet a file size requirement. Large image files slow down page load times, take up unnecessary storage, and often exceed attachment limits. Compressing them first solves all three problems.

The tool supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats and lets you preview the result before downloading. Adjust the quality slider to find the right balance between file size and visual quality for your specific use case.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will compressing my image reduce the quality?

Compression reduces file size by removing some image data. At moderate settings (70–85% quality), the difference is typically invisible to the naked eye. The preview lets you judge the quality before downloading.

What image formats does the compressor support?

Hephio's image compressor supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP files.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
