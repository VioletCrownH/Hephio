Frequently Asked Questions

Will converting my image reduce quality?

Converting to a lossy format like JPEG may reduce quality slightly depending on the quality setting. Converting to PNG preserves full quality. You can adjust the quality setting before downloading.

Why would I convert a PNG to WebP?

WebP files are typically 25–35% smaller than equivalent JPEG or PNG files at the same visual quality, making them ideal for web pages where file size affects load speed.

Is there a file size limit?

No server-side limits apply since conversion happens in your browser. Very large files may be affected by your device's available memory.
