Free Online Image Cropper & Rotator — No Upload Required

Crop and rotate images directly in your browser without sending your files to any server. Hephio's crop and rotate tool gives you simple, precise controls for trimming photos and correcting orientation, with complete privacy.

Cropping is one of the most common image tasks — removing unwanted backgrounds, preparing a profile photo to the right dimensions, or focusing on a specific subject. Rotation fixes photos taken at the wrong angle, which is especially common with scanned documents and smartphone photos.

Select your crop area visually, choose a rotation angle or flip direction, and download the result instantly. No software to install, no account to create.

Key Features

  • Drag-to-select crop area with pixel-precise control
  • Rotate images 90°, 180°, or by custom angle
  • Flip horizontally or vertically
  • Lock crop to specific aspect ratios
  • Browser-based — no uploads, fully private
  • Free, no account required

Frequently Asked Questions

Will cropping reduce image quality?

No. Cropping simply removes pixels outside the selected area. The remaining image retains its original quality.

Can I crop to a specific aspect ratio like square or 16:9?

Yes. You can lock the crop area to a specific aspect ratio for consistent output dimensions — useful for profile photos, thumbnails, and banners.

Can I undo my crop before saving?

Yes. You can adjust the crop selection and rotation at any time before downloading the final result.
