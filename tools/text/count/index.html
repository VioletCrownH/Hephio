Frequently Asked Questions

Does the counter show characters with and without spaces?

Yes. Both are displayed separately — useful for platforms like Twitter that count characters with spaces, and for character limits that exclude spaces.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated based on an average adult reading speed of approximately 200–250 words per minute.

Is there a maximum text length?

No. The counter handles any length of text without limitations.
