Free Strong Password Generator — Customizable & Completely Private

Generate strong, random passwords instantly in your browser. Hephio's password generator runs entirely locally — no passwords are ever sent to a server, logged, or stored anywhere. What you generate stays between you and your device.

Strong passwords are the first line of defense against unauthorized account access. A secure password is long, random, and unique to each account. Reusing passwords — even strong ones — across multiple accounts is one of the most common causes of account compromise. A generator makes it easy to create a different strong password for every account.

Choose your password length, select which character types to include, and generate as many as you need. Copy to clipboard in one click.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the generated passwords truly random?

Yes. The tool uses your browser's built-in cryptographically secure random number generator (the Web Crypto API) — the same standard used by security applications.

Are my passwords stored anywhere?

No. Nothing is sent to a server or stored anywhere. The passwords exist only in your browser until you copy them and close the page.

How long should my passwords be?

Security experts recommend at least 16 characters for important accounts. Passwords of 20+ characters with mixed character types are significantly harder to crack and are recommended for financial and email accounts.
