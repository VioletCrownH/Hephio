Free PDF Password Protection — Secure Your Documents Before Sharing

Add password protection to any PDF file directly in your browser, without uploading your document to a server. Hephio's PDF protect tool encrypts your PDF locally on your device, meaning sensitive content never passes through an external service — a critical distinction when protecting legal, financial, or personal documents.

Password-protecting a PDF matters when sharing sensitive documents by email, storing confidential files in shared cloud folders, or distributing content that should only be accessed by specific people. Encryption is a meaningful layer of security before documents leave your hands.

Enter your chosen password, apply the protection, and download the encrypted PDF. Anyone who opens the file will need the password you set.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of encryption does the tool apply?

The tool applies standard PDF encryption compatible with all major PDF readers including Adobe Acrobat, Preview on Mac, and others.

Can I remove a password from a PDF using this tool?

The Protect tool adds passwords. To remove protection, open the PDF in your PDF reader with the current password, then save or export without password protection applied.

What if I forget the password I set?

There is no way to recover a forgotten PDF password. Store your password in a password manager or secure location before protecting your documents.
