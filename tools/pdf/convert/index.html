Frequently Asked Questions

Will the formatting be preserved after conversion?

Complex PDF layouts may not convert perfectly to editable formats. Simple text-based documents typically convert cleanly. The output quality depends on how the original PDF was created.

Is my PDF data secure during conversion?

Yes. All conversion happens entirely in your browser. Your documents are never sent to any server or third party.

Why do some PDFs not convert well?

PDFs created from scans (images of text) don't contain actual text data, making them harder to convert accurately. PDFs created digitally from Word or similar tools convert much more cleanly.
