Free PDF Page Rotation — Fix Sideways or Upside-Down Pages

Fix the orientation of PDF pages without sending your file to a server. Hephio's PDF rotation tool lets you rotate individual pages or the entire document 90°, 180°, or 270°, directly in your browser.

Scanned documents frequently come out sideways or upside down depending on how they were fed into the scanner. Rotating pages in a PDF is one of the most common fixes needed before sharing a scanned document. Getting it right means the recipient can read the file without awkwardly turning their head or screen.

Select which pages to rotate, choose the rotation angle, and download the corrected file. The tool processes everything locally and works on any PDF regardless of file size.

Key Features

  • Rotate individual pages or all pages at once
  • Rotation options: 90°, 180°, 270° clockwise or counterclockwise
  • Visual page preview to confirm orientation before saving
  • No file size limits
  • Browser-based — no uploads required
  • Free, no account needed

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rotate just one page in a multi-page PDF?

Yes. You can select specific pages to rotate while leaving all other pages in their original orientation.

Does rotating a page affect the text or images?

No. Rotating only changes the viewing orientation of the page. All text, images, and other content remain completely intact.

Will the rotation be saved permanently?

Yes. The downloaded PDF will have the new orientation permanently saved. It will open correctly in any PDF viewer.
