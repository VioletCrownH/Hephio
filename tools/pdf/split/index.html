Free PDF Splitter — Split Into Pages or Custom Ranges

Split a PDF into individual pages or custom page ranges directly in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server — your PDF is processed locally on your device, keeping sensitive documents completely private.

Splitting PDFs is useful when you need to share a specific section of a larger document, separate a multi-section report into individual files, extract a contract or invoice from a compiled PDF, or pull out specific pages without revealing the rest of the document.

Choose to split every page into its own file, or define custom page ranges for precise control. Download all split files at once in a ZIP, or select just the pages you need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract just specific pages — not every page?

Yes. You can define custom page ranges to extract exactly the pages you need rather than splitting every page into a separate file.

Will splitting a PDF affect the quality of the content?

No. Splitting simply separates pages without modifying or re-encoding any content. Quality is fully preserved.

What's the difference between Split and Extract?

Split divides a PDF into multiple separate files (either every page, or defined ranges). Extract creates one new document containing only the pages you select.
