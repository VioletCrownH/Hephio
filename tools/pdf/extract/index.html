Free PDF Page Extractor — Pull Out Specific Pages Instantly

Extract specific pages from a PDF and save them as a new document — entirely within your browser. No file is ever uploaded to a server, so sensitive documents stay completely private on your device.

Extracting pages is useful when you need to share a specific section of a larger document, pull out an individual contract or invoice from a compiled PDF, or separate one chapter from a multi-chapter file without giving someone access to the full document.

Enter the page numbers or ranges you want to extract and the tool creates a new PDF containing only those pages, ready to download immediately.

Key Features

  • Extract single pages or custom page ranges
  • Supports non-consecutive page selections (e.g. pages 1, 3, 7–10)
  • Creates a new PDF — original file is unchanged
  • No file size limits
  • Browser-based — files never leave your device
  • Free, no account required

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract non-consecutive pages?

Yes. You can specify individual page numbers or ranges, including non-consecutive selections like pages 1, 3, and 7–10.

Is the extracted content preserved at full quality?

Yes. Extracted pages retain exactly the same quality as the original document. No re-encoding occurs.

What's the difference between Extract and Split?

Extract creates one new document from your selected pages. Split divides a PDF into multiple separate files — either every page or defined ranges.
