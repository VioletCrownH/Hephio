Privacy Policy

How Hephio handles browser-based processing, analytics, and basic site data.

Effective date: March 10, 2026

Hephio provides browser-based tools for working with images, PDFs, text, files, and related content. This Privacy Policy explains what information Hephio does and does not collect when you use the site.

1. Browser-Based Processing

Hephio tools are designed so that processing happens locally in your browser whenever possible. Files and text you use with Hephio tools are not uploaded to Hephio servers for processing.

As a result, Hephio does not receive, store, or review the contents of files, images, PDFs, or text you process using the tools.

2. Information Hephio Does Not Collect

Hephio does not intentionally collect the contents of files or text you process with the tools, including:

  • uploaded files
  • file contents
  • file names
  • text entered into tools
  • PDF contents
  • image contents or metadata submitted for tool processing

3. Analytics

Hephio uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site and improve the design and usefulness of its tools.

Analytics data may include information such as:

  • page views
  • general device and browser information
  • approximate geographic region
  • referring websites or sources
  • basic interaction events, such as when a tool is used

Hephio does not send file contents, filenames, entered text, hashes, encoded strings, PDF text, or similar user-generated tool data to Google Analytics.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to measure traffic and site usage. Those technologies are controlled by Google Analytics and related browser settings.

5. User Communications

If you contact Hephio by email or provide feedback through other communication channels, Hephio may retain the information you voluntarily provide in order to respond to your message or improve the site.

This information is not shared with third parties except as necessary to respond to your request.

6. Third-Party Services

Hephio may rely on third-party services for analytics, hosting, or infrastructure. Those providers may process limited technical information necessary to provide their services.

At launch, the primary third-party analytics service used by Hephio is Google Analytics 4.

7. Data Security

Because Hephio tools process files locally in the browser, the site is built to minimize the amount of user content sent to external systems. However, no website or internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Hephio is not directed to children under 13, and Hephio does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

Hephio may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact Hephio at support@hephio.com.
